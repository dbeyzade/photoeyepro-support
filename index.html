FAQ

Common questions.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open PhotoEye Pro → on the paywall screen, tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is there a subscription?

No. PhotoEye Pro uses a one-time, lifetime purchase model at $19.99. Pay once, use forever — no recurring charges.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Yes. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in with the same account.

What iOS version is required?

PhotoEye Pro requires iOS 16 or later.

Does the app collect my photos?

No. All processing happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any photos or personal data.

What is Spectral Vision?

Spectral Vision simulates infrared (>700nm) and ultraviolet (<400nm) imaging using Core Image GPU filters. It's an artistic and scientific visualization tool — not actual IR/UV hardware.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

No Data Collection

PhotoEye Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All camera processing, filters, and effects run entirely on your device using Apple's Core Image and Metal frameworks.

Camera & Photos Access

The app requests access to your camera and photo library solely to provide its core functionality. Photos are never uploaded or shared with any server.

Purchase Data

In-app purchase status is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase status is stored locally on your device via Apple's transaction system.

No Third-Party SDKs

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. There are no third-party libraries that could collect data about you.

Local Storage Only

App preferences (onboarding status, purchase status, launch count) are stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.
